The Credible Collector


Creddy the Credble Collector is a reasearch paper aggregator and data visualisation tool. He makes an api call to retrieve a number of research papers based on a keyword, and returns data fitting various criteria, such as: title, author, date published, and number of times cited. Currently he can only access one database, the smithsonians ADS, but the hope is that one day he can gain access to many different databases depending the topic. These papers are then graphed with size being based on number of citations, and the connection based on the publishing date of the paper. The hope is that in the future connections can be made more dynamically to enable better research techniques for students.
